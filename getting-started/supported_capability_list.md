```text
chown
dac_override
dac_read_search
fowner
fsetid
kill
setgid
setuid
setpcap
linux_immutable
net_bind_service
net_broadcast
net_admin
net_raw (supported)
ipc_lock
ipc_owner
sys_module
sys_rawio
sys_chroot
sys_ptrace
sys_pacct
sys_admin
sys_boot
sys_nice
sys_resource
sys_time
sys_tty_config
mknod
lease
audit_write
audit_control
setfcap
mac_override
mac_admin
```
